# GeekBrain — Company Overview

## Mission

GeekBrain's mission is to make digital commerce accessible to every merchant in Southeast Asia by providing reliable, intelligent payment infrastructure. We remove the technical barriers that prevent small and mid-sized merchants from accepting modern payments at scale.

## History

GeekBrain was founded in 2021 in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, by a team of engineers and product leaders with backgrounds in banking technology and e-commerce logistics. The company was built on the observation that SE Asian merchants were underserved by legacy payment rails and lacked affordable fraud protection.

- **2021**: Founded. Initial product: a basic payment gateway for Vietnamese merchants.
- **2022**: Launched GeekShield (fraud detection) as an integrated add-on. First 500 merchant customers.
- **2023**: Expanded to Thailand and Indonesia pilot markets. Launched GeekCommerce (order management platform). Series B funding closed.
- **2024**: Raised $45M Series C led by regional growth investors. Headcount grew from ~80 to ~200 employees. Began integrating ML-driven fraud scoring into the core product.
- **2025**: Achieved significant transaction volume milestones. Deepened bank API partnerships across Vietnam (VCB, Techcombank, BIDV).

## Product Lines

### GeekPay
The payment gateway product. Processes credit card transactions and bank transfers for e-commerce merchants. Supports major Vietnamese banks and select regional banks. Offers both hosted checkout and API-first integration options.

### GeekCommerce
An order management platform that handles the full order lifecycle — from creation through inventory validation, payment collection, fulfillment coordination, and completion. Designed for merchants with moderate to high order volume who need more than a basic storefront.

### GeekShield
Real-time fraud detection powered by machine learning. Scores every transaction before it is committed, giving merchants configurable risk thresholds. The model is trained on GeekBrain's own transaction history and updated periodically to adapt to new fraud patterns.

## Market Position

GeekBrain competes in the SE Asian payments and commerce enablement space, targeting merchants in Vietnam primarily and expanding into neighboring markets. Our differentiation is the integrated stack — payment processing, order management, and fraud detection working together — rather than point solutions that merchants must stitch together themselves.

## Headcount
